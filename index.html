BusMall Catalog Market Research

The goal of this exercise is to gauge your preferences on a variety of products for a new BusMall Catalog. We are conducting market research through voluntary participation in user focus groups.

Instructions:

  • You will be shown 3 products at a time. You must choose 1 product you are most likely to purchase.
  • Please answer by clicking on the image of the product.
  • After 25 rounds, your results will be displayed.

    • Which product are you most likely to buy today?
